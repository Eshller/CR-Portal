ABOUT US

Abhyuday, IIT Bombay is a student-run organisation working towards creating a sense of Social Responsibility and Leadership among the youth of India.

WHAT IS CR PROGRAM?

The College Representative program of Abhyuday, IIT Bombay is a student-led initiative promoting social change among college students in India. Representatives raise awareness, organize events, and mobilize students to address issues like education, health, and gender equality. The program provides leadership training, resources, and a network for impactful community engagement. It empowers students to create positive change while fostering empathy and understanding of social challenges.

FAQs

  • What can be the size of the team ?-
    Minimum 2 - maximum 4
  • Should all the team members need to be from the same college ?
    Yes, team members should be from the same college
  • If I'm the only person from my college can I still participate in the program ?
    Yes! You can participate. You can continue as an individual.
  • Can I add teammates who have not registered on the portal ?
    No! You can add only those in your team who have registered on the portal
  • How long will this tenure be ?
    The official tenure will range from around 6 months. You may take a break in between if you need so. This is the time when the CR is expected to complete the tasks given and claim the rewards and incentives we provide.
  • How much time do I have to invest ?
    On an average, 1 to 2 hours of work weekly would suffice. You can choose your own hours!
  • How will I get my incentives ?
    The majority of incentives will be shared via online mode and in div cases, we might courier it to your address.
  • Can I work from home ?
    Yes,you can work from home
  • What is the eligibility criteria for becoming CR ?
    Students from any college, stream, year of college can register for the CR Program.
